## Buyer's Guide

**5-inch vs 6-inch:** 5-inch pads are more common and have more accessory options. 6-inch covers more area but is harder to control in tight spaces.

**Orbit size:** Larger orbit (3/32 inch) removes material faster but leaves slightly more marks. Smaller orbit (1/8 inch) is better for finish work.

**Dust collection:** Always connect to a shop vac for best results. Built-in bags fill quickly on large projects.
