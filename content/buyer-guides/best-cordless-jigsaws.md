## Buyer's Guide

**Blade types:**
- **T-shank**: Universal standard — fits most modern jigsaws
- **U-shank**: Older standard — fewer blade options, check compatibility

**Orbital settings explained:**
- **Setting 0 (straight cut)**: Cleanest cut, slowest material removal — use for finish cuts, tile, metal
- **Setting 1–2**: Light orbital action — general wood cutting
- **Setting 3–4**: Aggressive orbital action — fast rough cuts in thick wood

**Stroke length:** Longer stroke (1 inch+) cuts faster. 3/4-inch stroke is adequate for most home use.
