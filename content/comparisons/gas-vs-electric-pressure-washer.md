## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is 2,000 PSI enough for a driveway?**
For a moderately soiled residential driveway, 2,000 PSI is workable but may require more passes and detergent. For heavily stained or oil-soaked concrete, 3,000+ PSI delivers faster, better results.

**Can an electric pressure washer replace a gas one?**
For most homeowner tasks — cleaning decks, siding, vehicles, and patios — a quality electric pressure washer like the Sun Joe SPX3000 is entirely adequate. For heavy commercial-scale cleaning, gas remains superior.

**How long does the Sun Joe SPX3000 motor last?**
With proper care (flushing after use, protecting from freezing), the SPX3000 motor can last 5-10 years for typical homeowner use. Avoid running it without water and store it properly through winter.

**Do gas pressure washers need winterization?**
Yes. Before winter storage, run a fuel stabilizer through the engine, then run the machine dry to prevent fuel from gumming up the carburetor. Also drain any remaining water from the pump to prevent freeze damage.
