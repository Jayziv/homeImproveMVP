## Frequently Asked Questions

**Are Milwaukee and DeWalt batteries interchangeable?**
No. Milwaukee M18 batteries only work with Milwaukee M18 tools, and DeWalt 20V MAX batteries only work with DeWalt 20V tools. The voltages are also technically different (DeWalt "20V MAX" = 18V nominal).

**Which brand holds its value better?**
Both hold their value well. Used Milwaukee M18 FUEL tools often command higher resale prices on secondary markets due to their professional-grade reputation.

**Can I use DeWalt bits with a Milwaukee drill?**
Yes — drill bits are universal. Any 1/2-inch chuck drill (DeWalt, Milwaukee, Makita, etc.) accepts the same standard round-shank or hex-shank bits.

**Is Milwaukee worth the extra money?**
For professional daily use, yes. Milwaukee FUEL tools deliver measurably better performance in demanding conditions. For home DIY use, DeWalt delivers 90%+ of Milwaukee's capability at a significantly lower price.
