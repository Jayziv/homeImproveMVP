## Performance

In our testing, the DCD777C2 drills through:

- **Hardwood (oak):** Good — requires slow speed and proper bit; no bogging

### Driving

The 15-clutch positions provide excellent torque control for driving screws:
- **#6 drywall screws:** Set to position 3–5, drives flush consistently
- **3-inch deck screws:** Position 10–12 handles these well in pine

## Battery Performance

The 1.5Ah batteries are the compromise in this kit. They're adequate for light-to-medium tasks but run low faster than the 2.0Ah batteries included in higher-end kits:

- **Light use (hanging pictures, assembling furniture):** 1–2 hours per charge
- **Medium use (building shelves, deck work):** 30–60 minutes per charge
- **Heavy use (continuous drilling):** 20–30 minutes

The charger tops up a 1.5Ah battery in approximately **35 minutes**. Having two batteries in the kit effectively doubles your working time.

**Upgrade recommendation:** If you plan heavy use, a pair of DCB203 2.0Ah batteries ($35–45) provides significantly better runtime.
