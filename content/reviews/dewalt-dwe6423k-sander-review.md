## Performance

### Variable Speed
The 8,000–12,000 OPM range is genuinely useful:
- **8,000 OPM:** Final finish passes on fine wood, applying wax or conditioner
- **10,000 OPM:** General sanding, prep coats between finish coats
- **12,000 OPM:** Aggressive material removal, paint stripping, leveling surfaces

Most sanders offer variable speed — the DEWALT dial is smooth and holds its setting reliably.

### Material Removal
On pine, using 80-grit, the DWE6423K removes approximately 3–4 grams of material per minute at full speed. This is competitive with the Makita BO5041 (similar motor class).

### Finish Quality
At 12,000 OPM with 220-grit, the 1/8-inch orbit leaves minimal swirl marks. On oak and maple, we achieved ready-to-finish surfaces in 15–20 minutes of sanding per square foot.

### Vibration
The DWE6423K vibrates more than the Bosch ROS20VSC at equivalent speeds. For 30-minute or shorter sessions, it's not an issue. For 1+ hour sessions, consider the Bosch if vibration fatigue is a concern.
