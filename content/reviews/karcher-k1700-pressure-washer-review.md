## The Vario Power Wand

The Karcher K1700's standout feature is its Vario Power (VPS) spray wand. Instead of swapping between 0°, 15°, 25°, and 40° nozzles, you twist the wand to adjust pressure continuously from gentle rinse to full power.

In practice: washing a car, you set low pressure for the body panels and high for the wheel wells — without stopping to change nozzles. For moving between a wood deck (low pressure) and concrete (high pressure) in the same session, it saves repeated nozzle changes.

**The Dirtblaster wand** (included) adds a rotating nozzle for stubborn grime — it concentrates pressure in a rotating pencil-stream pattern that cuts through moss, algae, and embedded concrete grime more effectively than a standard 15° nozzle.

## Performance

### Driveway Cleaning
At 1,700 PSI / 1.2 GPM (2,040 cleaning units), the K1700 handles light-to-medium concrete cleaning well. Light dirt, dust, and algae come off cleanly. Heavy oil stains and years of embedded grime require a higher-PSI model (the Karcher K5 or Sun Joe SPX3000).

For a driveway cleaned annually, the K1700 is sufficient. For a driveway that hasn't been cleaned in years, consider stepping up to 2,000+ PSI.

### Car Washing
The Vario Power wand is excellent for car washing — you can adjust down to a gentle rinse for paint-safe washing. At low pressure it cleans without risk of etching paint or trim.

### Deck and Patio
Good performance. The Dirtblaster wand is particularly effective on algae-covered composite deck boards and weathered wood.
